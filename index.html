Meet Farah from Madagascar

Before charity: water brought clean water to her village, 8-year-old Farah walked 3 hours every day to collect dirty water from a distant pond.

Today, she has clean water just steps from her home. Instead of walking for hours, she spends her time in school, learning to read and playing with friends.

Your donation doesn't just provide water—it gives children like Farah the chance to dream bigger.
